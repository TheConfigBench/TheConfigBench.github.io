About TheConfigBench

TheConfigBench is a YouTube channel built for entry to mid-level electronics technicians who want real, practical knowledge — not just theory on a whiteboard.

Every episode is filmed at a real workbench using real test equipment: Simpson 260 meters, a SIGLENT oscilloscope, Qucs for schematics, and a hands-on approach to every concept.

The companion textbook for this series is Complete Electronics Self-Teaching Guide by Boysen & Kybett — highly recommended.

Episodes follow a structured curriculum — Ohm's Law, Watt's Law, series/parallel circuits, transistor biasing, and beyond.

The Tuesdays with Dom sub-series (200-series episodes) documents weekly mentoring sessions with a 13-year-old learning electronics from scratch — great for younger viewers or complete beginners.

New episodes drop weekly. Giveaways with each major topic series.
